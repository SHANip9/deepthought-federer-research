# DeepThought Target Company Research Assignment

Submitted by Souradeep Ghosh.

This repository contains my completed target-company research assignment for the Business Analytics Internship at DeepThought. I selected one operating hub, screened companies against the Federer-company profile, scored the final 25, and documented the sourcing and scale-up approach.

## Chosen Scope

- City hub: Pune operational hub
- Segments: Industrial sensors & instrumentation; Precision auto components & engineering

I chose Pune because the Chakan, Bhosari, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Sanaswadi and Pirangut industrial belt has a strong concentration of mid-sized B2B manufacturers in precision engineering, auto components, instrumentation, machine tools and automation.

## Deliverables

- `data/federer_companies_pune.csv` - 25 scored companies with metrics, bands, source URLs and personalization hooks
- `data/federer_companies_pune.xlsx` - spreadsheet version of the same data plus audit and summary sheets
- `data/candidate_audit_log.csv` - rejected/deferred companies and reasons
- `docs/methodology.md` - sourcing, gating and scoring method
- `docs/part_b_sourcing_strategy_and_1000_company_proposal.md` - Part B strategy and scale-up proposal
- `docs/ai_prompts_and_code_notes.md` - research prompts and code notes
- `scripts/build_outputs.mjs` - output builder
- `scripts/score_validator.py` - score and gate validator
